Duddingston Library

Yr 4 Architecture Design

The project proposes a master plan for a school of regional studies in Duddingston, Scotland, based on local food initiatives. The plan aimed to bring good food education to the public, cut emissions from imported produce, and improve diets in Edinburgh.

Jade Eco Park

Yr 3 Architecture Design

This studio course delved into the depths of complex techno-environmental projects and critically examine their architectural precedents. Students were tasked with researching one project from a curated list and presenting their findings in a comprehensive visual report, featuring drawings, models, and accompanying text. The course culminated in a student-led adaptation of the chosen project, set in a future context that urgently addresses the pressing issue of climatic problems.

Waiting: Transit Nodes

Yr 4 Architecture Dissertation

This dissertation examines waiting for public transport, with a focus on pre-departure environments and the potential for waiting to be viewed as "incipient rich durations." Using David Bissell's framework of embodied activity and engagement, I investigate how transport infrastructure design can shape waiting environments. Through case studies, I showcase architectural solutions that challenge standardized typologies and offer creative engagement during waiting.
